01. The Problem

As an Operations Manager at a retail store, I handle two major shipments every week (Monday and Wednesday arrivals). Each manifest contains hundreds of SKUs and thousands of units. The challenges I faced daily included:

Manual manifest review was time-consuming and error-prone. Identifying new products, tracking quantities, and planning for incoming shipments required scrolling through lengthy PDFs and manually comparing data week over week.

  • No visibility into new products until they physically arrived, making it impossible to prepare locations or train staff
  • No historical comparison to understand volume trends or predict busy receiving periods
  • Task management scattered across notes, emails, and memory
  • Decision-making based on gut feeling rather than data-driven insights

02. The Solution

I built SwiftOps to automate the entire manifest processing workflow and provide actionable intelligence for better operational planning. The system:

Automated PDF Parsing

Upload manifest PDFs and automatically extract all SKUs, descriptions, quantities, and case packs using intelligent table detection.

New Product Detection

Instantly identifies products that have never been received before, allowing proactive preparation for new inventory.

Task Automation

Automatically creates tasks when new products are detected, with priority levels and due dates tied to arrival schedules.

AI-Powered Insights

Powered by SentinelAI, get intelligent analysis of manifests, trend identification, and planning recommendations.

Key Innovation: The AI assistant understands the context of inventory management and can answer questions like "What should I prioritize for Monday's truck?" or "Are there any concerning trends in recent shipments?"

03. Key Features

  • Dashboard Overview: At-a-glance stats showing upcoming arrivals, pending tasks, units expected, and total SKUs tracked
  • Manifest Management: Upload, process, and review manifests with detailed item breakdowns
  • Smart Product Tracking: Historical database of all products with first-seen dates and volume history
  • Task System: Create, prioritize, and track tasks with automatic creation for new product reviews
  • Analytics Dashboard: Top products by volume, new product tracking, and trend analysis
  • AI Assistant: Chat interface for getting insights and recommendations about inventory operations

05. Technical Implementation

SwiftOps is built as a full-stack web application with a focus on reliability and ease of use:

  • Backend: Python/Flask with SQLite database for persistent storage
  • PDF Processing: pdfplumber for intelligent table extraction with fallback text parsing
  • AI Integration: Connected to SentinelAI (self-hosted Ollama) for intelligent analysis
  • Frontend: Clean, responsive UI with real-time updates and intuitive workflows
  • Data Model: Normalized schema tracking manifests, products, items, tasks, and history
