Welcome to Histacruise

Your personal cruise journal, statistics dashboard, and social community — all in one place.

Track Every Voyage

Log each cruise with dates, ship, cruise line, region, cabin details, cost, and personal ratings. Upload photos and attach port itineraries with drag-and-drop ordering.

Detailed Statistics

Watch your numbers grow. See total days at sea, cruises by year, spending trends, favorite cruise lines, most-sailed ships, and cost breakdowns with interactive charts.

Interactive Cruise Map

Visualize every port you've visited on a world map. See your routes traced across the globe and discover how much of the world you've explored by sea.

Photo Galleries

Upload photos to each cruise and set cover images. Browse your memories in a built-in gallery — attach images when creating a cruise or add them later.

🏆

Badges & Achievements

Earn 12 unique badges as you cruise — from "First Voyage" to "Fleet Commander." Your trophy case is displayed on your profile for others to admire.

💬

Social Community

Share posts, photos, and cruise stories with fellow cruisers. React with hearts, follow other travelers, and discover new members in the community feed.

More to Explore

Cruise Passport

Your profile shows total voyages, days at sea, unique ships sailed, and regions explored — each with a fun title based on your experience level.

Budget Tracking

Set a yearly cruise budget and track spending in real-time. See cost per day, average per cruise, and breakdowns by cruise line.

Timeline View

See your complete cruise history laid out chronologically. Each card shows the ship, dates, region, and duration at a glance.
