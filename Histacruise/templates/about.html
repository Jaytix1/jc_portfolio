About Histacruise

Histacruise is a personal cruise tracking application designed to help you organize and analyze your cruising history.

What You Can Track:

  • 🚢 Cruise lines you've sailed with
  • ⚓ Ships you've been aboard
  • 📅 Sailing dates and cruise lengths
  • 🗺️ Regions and destinations visited
  • ⏱️ Total days spent cruising

As you add cruises to your history, Histacruise generates statistics that reveal your cruising patterns and preferences. Discover which cruise lines you favor, which regions you visit most, and track your maritime adventures over time.

Create your account today and start building your personal cruise history!
